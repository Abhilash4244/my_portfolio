Performance Analysis of Body Repair Workshop in Automobile Service Centers

About the Dataset :- Dataset contains workshop details, Estimation detials and Customer details table. It has data related to 15 body repair workshop and a total of 4021 records which represent individual estimations for customer vehicles.

Approach :- Revenue Oppurtunity, Conversion, Conversion % is shown using simple card visuals. Custom Visual Scroller is used to show company wise conversion percentage. Line and Clustered Column chart is used to show the trend of Revenue Oppurtunity and Conversion over the year. Drill down is enabled for Quartly metrics and Tooltip visual is provided for monthly trend. Sales Management Insights
